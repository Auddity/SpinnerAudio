Spinner is a recording facility located in the Reno Nevada area, nestled in Antelope Valley. Spinner provides professional audio recording, audio mixing, audio mastering, and instructional services.

services

audio recording, mixing, mastering & tape transfer

Spinner Audio is a recording studio located in Antelope Valley with a reputation of providing high quality music recording and production.

Using a combination of analog and digital, blending classic recording methods and the latest digital recording and editing techniques to create a second to none recording environment.

Accurate monitoring and great equipment are only part of the story here. The comfortable atmosphere is something that our clientèle enjoy and often comment on. We strive to maintain an intimate, personal feel.

instruction

Experienced in all forms of audio production, specializing in Cubase and Logic Pro.

Providing comprehensive instructions for using Cubase and Logic Pro to set up a recording system, compose music, edit audio and MIDI files, and output audio for CD productions.
